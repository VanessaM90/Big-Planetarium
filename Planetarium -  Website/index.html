Welcome to the Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It is a huge collection of stars, planets, dust, and gas held together by gravity.

Our planet, Earth, is only a tiny part of this enormous galaxy. By exploring space, we can learn more about where we came from and what exists beyond our world.

What is the Milky Way?

The Milky Way is a spiral galaxy. That means it has a bright centre and long arms that stretch outwards. Billions of stars are found inside it, including our Sun.
