About Us

Big Planetarium is a space education experience based in Bristol, designed to inspire curiosity about the universe in a fun, clear and engaging way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make astronomy accessible to everyone. We aim to help children, families and school groups enjoy learning about planets, stars and galaxies.

Accessibility

This website has been designed with accessibility in mind by using clear language, readable colours, descriptive images and simple navigation. It is also designed to be usable for people with different abilities, including those who rely on keyboard navigation or assistive technologies.
